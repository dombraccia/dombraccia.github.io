About me

I am currently a Bioinformatics Consultant contracting through C.M. Cherry Consulting.

Prior to that, I worked at mid-sized Boston startup as a Bioinformatics Data Scientist where I analyzed genomic, transcriptomic, and proteomic data in support of early Biomarker Discovery.

I received my Ph.D. from the University of Maryland where I built robust bioinformatic pipelines characterizing health relevant functions of the human gut microbiome.
